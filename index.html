Restaurants

Searching for tasty food is my go-to stress reliever during study sessions. Yet, my exploration of Ann Arbor's culinary scene barely scratches the surface, with only 1% of its total restaurants discovered so far. Here, I'll share a handful of dining spots that have truly impressed me, ones I'll undoubtedly revisit time and time again. If any of these are unfamiliar to you, rest assured, each one is well worth a taste!

MOLI Dancing Troupe

MOLI holds a special place in my heart - it's the one and only school club I've joined since my arrival. MOLI is a Chinese dancing troupe with a diverse repertoire spanning Chinese dance, K-pop, jazz, and choreography. This section summarizes the unforgettable performances, big and small, that I have shared with my fellow MOLI members. Oh, I really love this big family. :)

Activities

Besides focusing on my studies, I've made a concerted effort to engage in a variety of activities, many of which are organized by the International Student Center, to expand my social circle and meet new friendships. It's not only an excellent opportunity to enhance my English proficiency but also to deepen my understanding of American culture.
